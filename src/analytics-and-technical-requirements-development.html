Analytics and Technical Requirements Development

The analytics and technical direction is crucial for creating innovative products, involving studying client needs, analyzing data, and formulating project requirements.

Start a project

Key tasks

Researching client and market needs

Analysts conduct in-depth analysis of client needs and market trends to identify opportunities for developing new products or improving existing ones.

Formulating technical requirements

Based on the gathered data, analysts develop detailed technical requirements that serve as the basis for the development team. The technical specification includes descriptions of functionality, interfaces, performance requirements, and product security.

Collaboration with development team

Analysts closely collaborate with developers at all project stages, ensuring understanding and proper interpretation of technical requirements. They also participate in adapting the project if changes are needed.

Testing and quality control

After product development, analysts participate in the testing process to ensure that the final product meets all stated requirements and client expectations.

User experience evaluation

Analysts conduct UX/UI evaluation to ensure that the final product is not only functional but also user-friendly.

The Importance

This direction provides a deep understanding of client needs and the market, enabling the creation of products that not only meet current requirements but also adapt to future changes. Well-executed preliminary analysis and technical requirements development minimize the risks of errors during development, reduce time spent on product refinement, and increase client satisfaction.

We understand the importance of each stage of product development and strive for excellence at all levels, from analytics to delivering the finished solution to the client.
